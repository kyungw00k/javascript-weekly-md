**Could Array.prototype.flatten Become 'smoosh'?**

This week, TC39 member Michael Ficarra [suggested renaming the `flatten` method to `smoosh`](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-flatMap/pull/56) to avoid breaking sites using MooTools' incompatible `flatten` method. The dislike for 'smoosh' [went viral](https://twitter.com/search?q=smoosh&src=typd) and people rapidly suggested alternatives like `squash`, `flat` and `chain`, while André Staltz started [a campaign to ‘prevent smoosh’](https://github.com/staltz/prevent-smoosh)!

The current state of play is the TC39 committee (who work on the standardization of ECMAScript and [are actually really nice](https://twitter.com/maggiepint/status/971801704801886209)) are [keeping their options (and dialogue) open](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-flatMap/pull/56#issuecomment-371418649) while Brian Terlson reminds us *"TC39's active GitHub users are not always representative of the entire committee."*
