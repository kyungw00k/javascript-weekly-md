[Compromised eslint-scope npm Package Published](https://blog.npmjs.org/post/175824896885/incident-report-npm-inc-operations-incident-of) — Just over 24 hours ago, an npm account was compromised and a popular package (eslint-scope) was infected with malicious code. [Post mortem from ESLint here.](https://eslint.org/blog/2018/07/postmortem-for-malicious-package-publishes) The long and short of it is you’re recommended to review your own use of this module and to move to using 2FA on your own npm accounts. [Revoking npm tokens and clearing node_modules](https://gist.github.com/localjo/59bb12b7a4789e01e3782cf8a1031c87) may also be a good idea.
