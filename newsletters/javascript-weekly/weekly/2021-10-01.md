**QUICK BITS:**

- The (unofficial, though by now *de facto*) [JavaScript / JS logo](https://github.com/voodootikigod/logo.js) is 10 years old! It was officially unveiled at JSConf EU 2011.
- Mike Sherov [notes on Twitter](https://twitter.com/mikesherov/status/1443679254009483273) that while jQuery eventually peaked in usage in 2020, it's still going strong and we owe the project a debt of gratitude.
- The [latest Safari Technology Preview](https://webkit.org/blog/11975/release-notes-for-safari-technology-preview-133/) has enabled support for `SharedArrayBuffer`.
- The developer of [Colorette](https://github.com/jorgebucaran/colorette), a terminal text coloring library, [claimed his code had been improperly copied and rebranded](https://twitter.com/jorgebucaran/status/1441713235980275718) into another library called [nanocolors](https://github.com/ai/nanocolors) – this led to [a huge Hacker News discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=28659838), an interesting Babel PR to [replace chalk with nanocolors](https://github.com/babel/babel/pull/13783) and Sindre Sorhus creating [yoctocolors](https://github.com/sindresorhus/yoctocolors) which boasts being even faster than nanocolors(!) 😅
