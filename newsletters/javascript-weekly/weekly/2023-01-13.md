## Top Stories

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/cpress/image/upload/w_1280,e_sharpen:60,q_auto/y69gxuyrresunbix7hkw.jpg)

[The State of JS 2022](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/) — *The State of JS* is one of the JavaScript ecosystem’s most popular surveys and this time 39,471 folks took part giving us a snapshot of the tools, technologies, and language features people are using (or not using!) There’s a lot to go through, but here are some key points:

- top-level `await` is the most newly adopted feature recently.
- The [JavaScript / TypeScript balance](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/usage/#js_ts_balance) shows a majority of developers using TypeScript over JS.
- Express remains by far the most popular [backend framework](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/other-tools/#backend_frameworks) with Nest, Fastify, Strapi, and Koa following somewhat behind.
- Other interesting results can be found in [JS pain points](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/opinions/#top_js_pain_points), [what is currently missing from JS](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/opinions/#top_currently_missing_from_js), and the ['Awards'](https://2022.stateofjs.com/en-US/awards/) for stand out items (complete with snazzy visual effects).
