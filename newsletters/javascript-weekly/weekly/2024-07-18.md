[Exploring JS: JavaScript Books for Programmers](https://exploringjs.com/) — You’ll know Dr. Axel from his fantastic [blog posts](https://2ality.com/) over the years, or his once tenure as *JavaScript Weekly*’s editor, but he also has an impressive array of no-nonsense books that you can mostly read for free online, **including the newly updated [Exploring JavaScript (ES2024 Edition)](https://exploringjs.com/js/index.html),** [Deep JavaScript](https://exploringjs.com/deep-js/index.html), and [Tackling TypeScript](https://exploringjs.com/tackling-ts/index.html).
