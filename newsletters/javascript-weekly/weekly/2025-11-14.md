**IN BRIEF:**

- The TC39 committee behind ECMAScript is meeting for the 111th time next week and [has a packed agenda to discuss.](https://github.com/tc39/agendas/blob/main/2025/11.md) *(Thanks to [Rob Palmer](https://x.com/robpalmer2/status/1988171966616912360) for the heads up.)*
- For Stack Overflow, Tanya Janca shares [several tips for writing more secure JavaScript.](https://stackoverflow.blog/2025/10/15/secure-coding-in-javascript/)
- The co-founder of the fantastic [Val Town](https://www.val.town/) online JavaScript platform shares [a retrospective of its past three years](https://macwright.com/2025/11/11/val-town.html), as well as two roles they're hiring for.
- [Visual Types](https://types.kitlangton.com/) is a handy visually-oriented guide/tour of what types are and how they work, with a focus on TypeScript.

**RELEASES:**

- [Bun v1.3.2](https://bun.com/blog/bun-v1.3.2) – Isolated package installs, introduced as the default in v1.3, were causing issues with some existing projects, so now hoisted installs are back as the default for those. `bun install` also gets even faster and you can do CPU profiling by running with `--cpu-prof`.
- [Node.js v25.2.0 (Current)](https://nodejs.org/en/blog/release/v25.2.0) – Now with **[type stripping](https://nodejs.org/en/learn/typescript/run-natively) marked as stable.**
- [esbuild 0.27](https://github.com/evanw/esbuild/releases/tag/v0.27.0) – Popular bundler. A notable release for having breaking changes (so you're reminded to pin `esbuild` appropriately).
- [TanStack DB 0.5](https://tanstack.com/blog/tanstack-db-0.5-query-driven-sync), [Node.js v24.11.1 (LTS)](https://nodejs.org/en/blog/release/v24.11.1), [MikroORM 6.6](https://mikro-orm.io/blog/mikro-orm-6-6-released)
