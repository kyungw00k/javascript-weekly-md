😗 **And a weird note to end on..**

Have you ever wanted to program by *whistling?* Now you can. [Velato](https://velato.net/HandsFree/) is a JavaScript-inspired esoteric language designed to be written entirely by whistling and you can give it a go in your browser right now. I struggled with it, but you might have more luck (it doesn't seem to like Safari, for starters).

Velato was built by [Daniel Temkin](https://danieltemkin.com/), the author of [*Forty-Four Esolangs*](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262553087/forty-four-esolangs/), a new book, published by MIT Press, about an artist's take on creating esoteric programming languages.
