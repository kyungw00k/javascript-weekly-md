[Angular v15 Released](https://blog.angular.io/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8) — It may not be the newest shiny thing, but Angular is dependable, widely used, and well maintainted. [Announcing TypeScript 4.9](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/typescript/announcing-typescript-4-9/) — The latest version of the popular typed JavaScript superset and associated tooling gains the [`satisfies` operator](https://tomdohnal.com/posts/typescript-satisfies-keyword) for matching types without losing them, [auto-accessors](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/typescript/announcing-typescript-4-9/#auto-accessors-in-classes) in classes, a change to how NaN equality checks are done, plus editor tooling and perf improvements.

Daniel Rosenwasser (Microsoft)
