[What Are Wrapper Objects for JavaScript's Primitive Values?](https://2ality.com/2022/02/wrapper-objects.html) — Dr. Axel talks about how primitive types like boolean and string have associated wrapper classes (e.g. Boolean and String) and demonstrates the *value* (pun not originally intended!) they provide.

[How to Read the ECMAScript Specification](https://timothygu.me/es-howto/) — Each time there’s a new ECMAScript spec we link to it - such as [this draft ES2022 spec](https://tc39.es/ecma262/).. but who reads such a complex and intimidating document? This is a neat dig into how the spec ticks formatting wise and why the spec is worth a skim at the least.
