[I Don't Hate Arrow Functions (But..)](https://davidwalsh.name/i-dont-hate-arrow-functions) — Arrow functions (`=>`), as introduced in ES6, have generally been a much welcomed addition to JavaScript but Kyle Simpson reminds us they’re not suitable in *every* scenario and has created [an ESLint plugin](https://github.com/getify/eslint-plugin-proper-arrows) to help you keep a handle on their use.

There are also several key performance improvements, all covered in the post.
