[TypeScript 4.7 Beta Released](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/typescript/announcing-typescript-4-7-beta/) — ES module support for Node.js is now an official feature, and you can specify in `package.json` whether JS files are interpreted as ES modules or CommonJS modules. `.mjs` and `.cjs` extensions are also supported if you prefer. There’s also improved function inference, instantiation expressions, and you can use `typeof` on private fields. Final TS releases tend to follow on quickly from betas, so expect 4.7 final soon.

Daniel Rosenwasser (Microsoft)

[A 'Search Engine' for JavaScript Operators](https://joshwcomeau.com/operator-lookup/) — Quick: name as many operators as you can! Got to about ten or so? This site covers about *fifty* with a quick explanation of each.
