[Valtio 2.0: Proxy State Made Simple](https://valtio.dev/) — Turns objects into self-aware proxies so you can access state and subscribe to changes outside of components, add [computed properties](https://github.com/pmndrs/valtio/blob/main/docs/guides/computed-properties.mdx) and more. Designed for React and compatible with Suspense, but can also be [used with vanilla JS.](https://github.com/pmndrs/valtio?tab=readme-ov-file#use-it-vanilla) – [GitHub repo.](https://github.com/pmndrs/valtio)
