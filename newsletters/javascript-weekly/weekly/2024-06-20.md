## Top Stories

[The Results of the *State of JavaScript 2023* Survey](https://2023.stateofjs.com/en-US) — It feels odd including something about 2023 in June 2024, but the results of the major annual JavaScript developer survey are now out. It’s interesting to see [what features JS devs do and don’t use](https://2023.stateofjs.com/en-US/features/), [changes in library popularity over time](https://2023.stateofjs.com/en-US/libraries/#tools_arrows), [what build tools people are using](https://2023.stateofjs.com/en-US/libraries/build_tools/), the [divide between JavaScript and TypeScript usage](https://2023.stateofjs.com/en-US/usage/#js_ts_balance), and much more besides.
