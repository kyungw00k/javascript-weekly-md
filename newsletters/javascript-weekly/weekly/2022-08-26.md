When Alternative JS Runtimes Grow Up: [Deno Has Big Changes Ahead](https://deno.com/blog/changes), and the Creator of [Bun](https://bun.sh/) Forms [Oven](https://oven.sh/)

Two distinct stories that we felt deserved to be together as they provide a little look at the development of alternative server-side JavaScript runtimes that *aren't* Node.js and are both going after overlapping markets.

Deno's big news is that they're working hard on npm compatibility and Deno will be able to use *most* npm modules soon ([Deno 1.25](https://deno.com/blog/v1.25) has been released with a preview). Meanwhile, Jarred Sumner, the creator of Bun, has answered our pun prayers and formed [Oven](https://oven.sh/), a company to support Bun's development, and already raised $7m for it. The heat is, most certainly, on.

→ [deno.com/blog/changes](https://deno.com/blog/changes) → [oven.sh](https://oven.sh/)
