## 🧐 Learning web development with Dr. Axel

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/cpress/image/upload/w_240,e_sharpen:60,q_auto/lioslh725i8k02a34tyl.jpg)

Over the past couple of months the esteemed Dr. Axel Rauschmayer has been working on [a valuable series of beginner-friendly posts](https://2ality.com/archive.html#Tag=learning%20web%20dev) on a range of web development topics, with a heavy focus on JavaScript. He pitches the series as a way to teach people "*who have never programmed how to create web apps with JavaScript*".

These are all excellent primers/refreshers, and ideal for sharing with those early in their web development journey. Here are some of the highlights so far:

- Working with [numbers, variables, and functions](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-numbers-variables-functions.html), and [strings and methods.](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-strings-methods.html)
- [Booleans, comparisons and `if` statements](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-booleans-comparisons-if.html) and [loops.](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-loops.html)
- Digging into [plain objects](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-plain-objects.html), [`Array`s](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-arrays.html), [`Map`s](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-maps.html), and [exceptions.](https://2ality.com/2025/08/javascript-exceptions.html)
- An [introduction to frontend frameworks](https://2ality.com/2025/09/frontend-frameworks.html) using Preact.
- [Installing npm packages and bundling.](https://2ality.com/2025/09/npm-packages-bundling.html)
- [Promises and `async` functions.](https://2ality.com/2025/09/javascript-async.html)

P.S. [*Don't go JSON waterfalls..*](https://dontgojsonwaterfalls.com/)
