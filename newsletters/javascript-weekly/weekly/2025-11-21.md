**This week's TC39 meeting:** The Ecma TC39 committee (the group behind the design of ECMAScript / JavaScript) [met up for the 111th time this week](https://github.com/tc39/agendas/blob/main/2025/11.md) *(seen above)* to discuss language proposals. The meeting notes won't be published for a few weeks, but several proposals did see some progress:

- [Iterator Sequencing](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-iterator-sequencing) progressed to stage 4.
- [Joint Iteration](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-joint-iteration), [Iterator Join](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-iterator-join), and [Await dictionary of Promises](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-await-dictionary/) go stage 2.7.
- [`Error.captureStackTrace`](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-error-capturestacktrace), [`import .. with {type: "text"}`](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-import-text), and [`Object.keysLength`](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-object-keys-length) all progressed to stage 2.
- The [Intl Unit Protocol](https://github.com/sffc/proposal-intl-unit-protocol) also reached stage 1 to provide a way to annotate quantities with the units being measured.
- [Typed Array Find Within](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1RIhMpf4gY2wX0KZcmCUU6i9l9Ay7WBu0vY4vIsJUwTg/edit?slide=id.g38e87ed9df8_0_0#slide=id.g38e87ed9df8_0_0) progressed to stage 1. Think a native `indexOf`-type method for TypedArrays.

*Note: Learn more about [what the TC39 stages mean here.](https://tc39.es/process-document/)*

[Tooltip Components Should Not Exist](https://tkdodo.eu/blog/tooltip-components-should-not-exist) — Dominik challenges some common wisdom in his typically erudite fashion.
