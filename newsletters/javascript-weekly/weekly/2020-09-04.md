[Underscore.js 1.11.0: The Long Standing Functional Helper Library Goes Modular](https://underscorejs.org/#1.11.0) — *“Underscore!?”* I hear some of our longer serving readings exclaiming. Yes, it’s still around, still under active development, and still a neat project at a mere 11 years old. As of v1.11.0 every function is now in a separate module which brings treeshaking opportunities to all, but there’s also a monolithic bundle in ES module format for those who prefer that. [This article](https://juliangonggrijp.com/article/introducing-modular-underscore.html) goes into a lot more depth about [the new modular aspects.](https://juliangonggrijp.com/article/introducing-modular-underscore.html)
