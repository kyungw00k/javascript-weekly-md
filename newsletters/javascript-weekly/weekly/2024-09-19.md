Sasha Koss

[ts-remove-unused: Remove Unused Code from TypeScript Projects](https://github.com/line/ts-remove-unused) — A tool that can auto-fix unused exports (removing `export` from the declaration or the whole declaration based on its usage) and delete modules that have no referenced exports. [Knip](https://knip.dev/) is another mature tool in this space, though focused more on *detecting* the things you can remove.
