## 📒 Articles & Tutorials

[A Look at ECMAScript's Iterator Helper Methods](https://v8.dev/features/iterator-helpers) — [The proposal](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-iterator-helpers) is several years old, but now at stage 3 in TC39, *Iterator Helpers* are being implemented and ship with V8 12.2/Chrome 122. The helpers are functions like `.map`, `.filter`, `.take` and `.forEach` and are made available to any object with `Iterator.prototype` in its prototype chain.
