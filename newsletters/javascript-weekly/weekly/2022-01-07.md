Now it’s on the Web. And I’m still terrible at it. [Here's the JS source.](https://github.com/oklemenz/PrinceJS) As an aside, Jordan Mechner (the creator of Prince of Persia) wrote a fantastic [book about the creation of the game](https://jordanmechner.com/store/the-making-of-prince-of-persia/) that I hugely enjoyed a few years ago. It's well worth a read if the diaries of a game developer sound at all interesting to you.
