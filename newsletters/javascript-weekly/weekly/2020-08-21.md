[TypeScript 4.0 Released](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/typescript/announcing-typescript-4-0/) — The latest major version of TypeScript, the increasingly popular superset of JavaScript that adds optional static typing, is here. No major breaking changes, but a laundry list of tweaks and smaller new features, including:

- variadic tuple types
- editor and editor integration improvements
- labeled tuple elements
- short circuiting assignment operators (e.g. `a ||= b`)
- the [new TypeScript web site!](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)

Daniel Rosenwasser and the TypeScript Team

[Metaprogramming with Proxies](https://exploringjs.com/deep-js/ch_proxies.html) — Dr. Axel, the author of [Deep JavaScript](https://exploringjs.com/deep-js/), keeps releasing extra chapters of his book for free online, and this one goes as deep into the topic of object proxies as you could ever hope. Do you *need* to know all of this? No. Am I glad the knowledge exists if I ever need to know? Yes!
