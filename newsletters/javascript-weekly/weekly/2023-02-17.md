**IN BRIEF:**

- 🐒 The just released [Firefox 110 for Android](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/whats-new-firefox-android) now supports [Tampermonkey,](https://www.tampermonkey.net/) an extension for running JavaScript 'userscripts' on sites you visit.
- The *Angular* project [is taking steps to revamp its reactivity model](https://github.com/angular/angular/discussions/49090) to enable fine-grained change detection via signals.
- The latest beta of iOS and iPadOS 16.4 [supports the *Web Push* API](https://webkit.org/blog/13878/web-push-for-web-apps-on-ios-and-ipados/) for home screen webapps.
- 🐦 A fun Twitter thread where Qwik's Miško Hevery attempted to demonstrate [why `a = 0-x` is about 3-10x faster than `a = -x`](https://twitter.com/mhevery/status/1626002464469323777) before being told about [a flaw in his benchmark.](https://twitter.com/mhevery/status/1626259524930932745) There is still a performance difference, though.
- [▶️ The React.js documentary](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8pDqJVdNa44) we mentioned last week has now been released and it's a heck of a watch – you'll need 78 minutes of your time though.
