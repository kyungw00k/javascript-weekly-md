[Deno 1.0 Released](https://deno.land/v1) — Two years ago, Ryan Dahl, the creator of Node.js, gave a talk about [the 10 things he regretted about Node.js.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3BM9TB-8yA) At the same time, he introduced Deno, a prototype of a new, security-first, npm-less JavaScript runtime — now it's considered ready for the big time and, at a minimum, is worth having a quick play with. If you want to do a walkthrough tutorial to see if it’s to your taste or not, [this is a good one](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/the-deno-handbook/) by Flavio Copes.
