## ⚡️ A quick tip for Mac Users

If you're on macOS and have [Homebrew](https://brew.sh/) installed, run `brew cask install quicklook-json` and Quick Look (where you press the spacebar with a file highlighted in *Finder*) will let you quickly look into JSON files with the correct formatting:

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/cpress/image/upload/w_1280,e_sharpen:60/szuze8fumvkkaiailwga.jpg)

If you don't have Homebrew, you can [install quicklook-json manually](http://www.sagtau.com/quicklookjson.html), though I can't vouch for that directly.

There are a [lot of other Quick Look plugins](https://github.com/sindresorhus/quick-look-plugins) covering other formats too, such as Markdown or APKs, and even adding syntax coloring to previewed code.
