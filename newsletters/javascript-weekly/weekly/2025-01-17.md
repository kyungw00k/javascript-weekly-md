## 📒 Articles & Tutorials

[A Look at Regular Expression Pattern Modifiers](https://2ality.com/2025/01/regexp-modifiers.html) — You may be familiar with using *flags* to change the behavior of regexes, but Dr. Axel looks at [a proposal](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-regexp-modifiers) bringing a way to change regex flags *within* subexpressions (e.g. `/^[a-z](?-i:[a-z])$/i;`). It’s at stage 4 and should land in ECMAScript 2025.
