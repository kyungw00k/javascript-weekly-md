# JavaScript Weekly Issue 601: August 5, 2022

## Top Stories

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/cpress/image/upload/w_1280,e_sharpen:60/glelkm75qu8jwap5jmnl.jpg)

[JS1024 2022 Competition Winners](https://js1024.fun/results/2022) — JS1024 is a ‘code golfing’ contest where you get 15 days to create a JavaScript or GLSL program within 1024 bytes. This results in lots of creative ideas, complete with fantastic (non-minified) source to check out. The winning JS entry is [this quirky typing game](https://js1024.fun/demos/2022/18) and the winning shader-based entry is [a 3D tunnel experience.](https://js1024.fun/demos/2022/1) There’s much to learn here as these folks have used every trick in the book, including **compressing 1900+ English words in [a heavily commented entry that uses](https://js1024.fun/demos/2022/18/source) Brotli and a specially crafted WOFF2 font file(!)**

JS1024

[Docusaurus 2.0 Released](https://docusaurus.io/blog/2022/08/01/announcing-docusaurus-2.0) — Docusaurus powered sites are everywhere in the JS space (e.g. [the Babel docs](https://babeljs.io/docs/en/)) and after four years of effort, [version 2.0](https://github.com/facebook/docusaurus) of the popular React-powered documentation-focused static site generator is here. Rebuilt from scratch, it adds [MDX](https://github.com/mdx-js/mdx) support, a new plugin system, theming, dark mode, SPA navigation, offline support, and more.

Meta (Facebook)

[Announcing EdgeDB 2.0](https://www.edgedb.com/blog/edgedb-2-0) — Try the database that makes ORMs obsolete. EdgeDB’s TypeScript client has the simplicity of an ORM but the power of a full-fledged query language. And with the release of EdgeDB 2.0 it’s better than ever—read the announcement for details.

EdgeDB sponsor

[Electron 20: The Cross Platform Desktop App Framework](https://www.electronjs.org/blog/electron-20-0) — 20 sounds like a big, round release number but it’s mostly a typical release. Chromium is bumped up to v104, V8 to 10.4, and Node to 16.15.0. Windows gains an ‘immersive dark mode’ and Electron windows can now [‘float’ over full-screen apps panel-style](https://github.com/electron/electron/pull/34665).
