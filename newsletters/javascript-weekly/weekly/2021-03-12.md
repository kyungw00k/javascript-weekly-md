Quick Bits

- [esbuild 0.9](https://github.com/evanw/esbuild/releases/tag/v0.9.0), the high performance bundler and minifier, has been released with support for `package.json`'s new `exports` field and a few backwards incompatible changes.
- Some [updates from the 81st meeting](https://dev.to/hemanth/updates-from-the-81st-meeting-of-tc39-2b3p) of TC39. [Module fragments](https://github.com/littledan/proposal-module-fragments#motivation) and [Temporal](https://tc39.es/proposal-temporal/docs/) (now at stage 3) feel like two things we'll be hearing more about in future.
- Storybook has added [experimental support for Webpack 5.](https://storybook.js.org/blog/storybook-for-webpack-5/)
- We do a bad job of communicating this, but if you're into Node or React, bear in mind we put most of *those* links in [Node Weekly](https://nodeweekly.com/) and [React Status](https://react.statuscode.com/) — our sister newsletters :-)
